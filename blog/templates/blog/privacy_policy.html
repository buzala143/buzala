Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Buza is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and engage with our community of users and legal professionals.

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: When you register, you may provide personal details such as your name, email address, and any other information you choose to share.
  • User Content: Information you post, including legal questions and comments, which may contain personal information.
  • Data: Technical data about your use of our platform, including your IP address, browser type, and access times.

3. Your Rights

In accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the following rights:

  • The right to access your personal information.
  • The right to correct inaccurate information.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time (where applicable).

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your posts and comments will be visible to other users, including lawyers.
  • Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist with platform operations, who may have access to your information as needed.
  • Legal professional: We may disclose your information to legal proffessionals, where we are of the view that the proffessional will be in a position to assist you with your post.
  • Legal Compliance We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to legal requests.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access and misuse. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the revised policy on our platform. Your continued use of our services after any modifications signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
